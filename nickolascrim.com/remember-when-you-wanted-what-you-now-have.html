Remember When You Wanted What You Now Have

I don’t remember where I heard/ read this, but it had an impact on me. So recently I went through the mental exercise and reflected on a time when I wanted what I now have- a relationship, job, financial goal.

The exercise reminded me of the material things, opportunities, and relationships I wanted and now had! Just with food, clothing, shelter, and family I have been given a lot. Yet I still struggle with contentment. You have to work at contentment and I have a lot of work to do!

Then I thought about reaching a level of gratefulness where I would be comfortable if all the blessings went away. I asked myself if I would find peace in knowing there was a time when I didn't have those things (I simply wanted them) and be thankful for the time I had them. To me that is true gratefulness and something I'm striving for!

We aren't promised tomorrow and life is too short to NOT be thankful for what we've been given. The things at one time we didn’t have but wanted!
